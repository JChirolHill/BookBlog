Rating Scale

Ratings for books are on a one to five scale, represented by the number of .
Here is a breakdown of the meanings:

  • Regretted reading, unpleasant read where you were better off just skipping it
  • Alright, the books that weren't bad enough to stop, but difficult or boring to get through
  • Pleasant read, may have been slow at some parts, but overall a good book
  • Really enjoyed, books that were great reads, but not necessarily life-changers
  • Favorites, books that stay with you your whole life
